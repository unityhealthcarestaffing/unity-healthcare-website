Who we are

A values-led healthcare staffing partner in Wales

Unity Healthcare Staffing Ltd is led by an experienced healthcare worker and chartered management professional, combining frontline care experience with strong governance, financial oversight and a genuine passion for quality.

Our mission

To support health and social care providers with reliable, well-presented and well-supported staff, so that residents, patients and service users receive safe, compassionate and consistent care – every shift.

Our values

  • Integrity – transparent, honest and accountable in all we do.
  • Compassion – treating every person with dignity and respect.
  • Excellence – high standards in recruitment, training and performance.
  • Unity – working in partnership with providers, staff and communities.

How it works

Simple, clear and compliant process

We keep things straightforward for busy managers while maintaining robust checks, training and documentation behind the scenes.

1

Tell us what you need

Share your service type, shift patterns, skill mix and any specialist needs. We agree rates and terms up front – no surprises.

2
